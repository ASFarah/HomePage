Meeting



Placement Problem in Context of Fog and Edge computing




The meeting will take place at Centre de recherche Inria Rhône-Alpes, Antenne Inria GIANT Minatec Campus, the 19th and 20th of June 2018. The meeting aims to review the challenges and open issues related to service placement problem (models, resolution strategies, programming platforms...) in Fog/Edge Environment.


Coordinator : Farah AIT SALAHT (INRIA-Grenoble), Adrien LEBRE (IMT Atlantique / Inria / LS2N), Frédéric DESPREZ (INRIA senior researcher)

Keywords: Fog/Edge Computing, Internet of Things, Service Placement, Optimization, Programming tools and plateforms
